# LC42 - Trapping Rain Water

## Pattern

Two Pointers (Opposite Direction)

---

## Search Space

Bars whose trapped water has not yet been finalized.

---

## Bottleneck

The smaller boundary determines the water level.

---

## Invariant

Processed bars already have their final trapped water.

---

## Decision Rule

Process the side with the smaller boundary.

---

## Proof

Once the smaller boundary is known, future information cannot increase the water above that bar.

---

## Common Mistake

Memorizing the algorithm instead of understanding why one side becomes final.
