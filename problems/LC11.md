# LC11 - Container With Most Water

## Pattern

Two Pointers (Opposite Direction)

---

## Search Space

All possible containers.

---

## Bottleneck

The smaller wall determines the maximum possible water.

---

## Invariant

The optimal container always exists between Left and Right.

---

## Decision Rule

Move the smaller wall.

---

## Proof

Moving the taller wall decreases width while leaving the bottleneck unchanged.

Therefore area cannot improve.

---

## Common Mistake

Thinking the taller wall should move because it is larger.
